Use Case Breakdown

The Pugsley excels in environments that defeat conventional MTBs:

  1. Snow & Winter
  2. Floatation on 3.7–4.3″ tires; ideal for Arrowhead 135, Iditarod Trail Invitational

  3. Studded tires (e.g. 45NRTH Dillinger) for ice

  4. Sand & Beach

  5. Loose beach sand, desert washes → 3.8–4.3″ tires at low PSI

  6. Front/tip tire swaps (4.8″ Bud/Lou up front on Moonlander fork)

  7. Expedition/Touring

  8. Global 26″ parts availability; Braze-ons for racks, cages

  9. Documented trips: Andes, Mongolia, Antarctica (e.g. Joe Cruz’s tours)

  10. Trail Riding

  11. Passive suspension from big tires; occasional 29″ conversion for speed

  12. Fun traction on roots/rocks but heavy for tight singletrack

  13. Utility & Cargo

  14. Hauling gear, groceries, firewood in winter; BOB trailers on snow
  15. Bike‐tram “snow tractor” setups
